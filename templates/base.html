Disclaimer

The AI Political Bias Analyzer is an experimental, research-oriented tool that attempts to estimate the perceived political leaning (Left, Center, or Right) of a given text or news article.

It uses a zero-shot machine-learning model (facebook/bart-large-mnli) with custom prompts. Results are not definitive, objective, or scientifically validated measures of bias. Political leaning is inherently subjective, context-dependent, and varies across cultures, time periods, and individuals.

Key limitations:

  • The model can be influenced by wording, tone, topic selection, and framing, even when the underlying facts are neutral.
  • It may misclassify satire, quotes, historical texts, or nuanced positions.
  • Confidence scores reflect the model’s internal certainty, not real-world accuracy.
  • The tool does not evaluate factual accuracy, only perceived ideological direction.
  • Short texts or texts with little explicit political content often produce unreliable or near-random results.

This tool is provided for educational, entertainment, and research purposes only. It should not be used as evidence in arguments, journalism, legal proceedings, or to label any person, organization, or publication as definitively “biased.”

We do not endorse or oppose any political ideology, party, or candidate. The developers of this tool may hold personal political views that differ from the model’s outputs.

Results can and will change as we experiment with prompts, models, and averaging techniques. Use critically and have fun exploring!
